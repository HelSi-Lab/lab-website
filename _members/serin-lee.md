Serin Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial & Management
Engineering at Incheon National University. She develops simulation, optimization,
and digital-twin methods for healthcare and infectious-disease systems.

She earned her Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from the University of Washington
in 2024, with a dissertation on *"Modeling Healthcare Policy: From Calibration to
Optimization."* Before joining INU, she was a postdoctoral research fellow at Fred
Hutchinson Cancer Center and a research scientist at the Bill & Melinda Gates
Foundation's Institute for Disease Modeling.

**Research interests:** healthcare systems, modeling & simulation, simulation
optimization, digital twin, agent-based models, pharmacometrics, model
calibration, and disease modeling.
