Serin Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial & Management Engineering at Incheon National University and the director of the Healthcare & Simulation Optimization (HelSi) Lab. Her research develops simulation, optimization, and digital-twin methods to support decision-making in complex healthcare, infectious disease, and biomanufacturing systems.

## Education

* **Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering**, University of Washington, 2024
  Dissertation: *Modeling Healthcare Policy: From Calibration to Optimization*
* **M.S. in Industrial Engineering**, University of Washington, 2021
* **B.S. in Industrial Engineering**, Seoul National University, 2018

## Work Experience
* **Assistant Professor**, Department of Industrial & Management Engineering, Incheon National University, 2025–Present
* **Postdoctoral Research Fellow**, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center, 2024–2025
* **Research Scientist**, Institute for Disease Modeling, Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, 2023–2024
* **Summer Research Intern**, Stanford University, 2021

## Research Interests

* Healthcare modeling and simulation
* Simulation optimization and digital twins
* Agent-based modeling
* Infectious disease policy modeling
* Pharmacometric and clinical-trial simulation
