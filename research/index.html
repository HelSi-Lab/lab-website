Research

Research Areas

HelSi Lab develops simulation, optimization, and digital-twin methods to support decision-making in complex bio/healthcare systems.

01

Healthcare Systems & Policy

We develop simulation and mathematical modeling approaches to support healthcare decision-making, including disease forecasting, health policy evaluation, and resource allocation.

02

Biopharmaceutical Modeling & Clinical Trials

We use pharmacokinetic modeling, clinical trial data, and simulation to evaluate drug adherence, treatment effectiveness, and trial design in biopharmaceutical applications.

03

Digital Twin & Simulation

We build digital-twin and simulation models for complex bio/healthcare systems, including virtual clinical trials, biomanufacturing processes, and healthcare operations.

04

Simulation Optimization & Explainable AI

We develop optimization, surrogate modeling, parameter calibration, and explainable AI methods to analyze complex simulations and support decision-making under uncertainty.
