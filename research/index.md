          evaluation, and resource allocation.</p>

        <p>We use pharmacokinetic modeling, clinical trial data, and simulation to evaluate drug adherence, treatment effectiveness, and trial design in biopharmaceutical applications.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="research-area-img"><img src="images/proj-dbs.jpg" alt="Biopharmaceutical modeling and clinical trial analysis"></div>

      <div class="research-area-text">
        <h2>Digital Twin &amp; Simulation</h2>
        <p>We build digital-twin and simulation models for complex bio/healthcare
          systems, including virtual clinical trials, biomanufacturing processes,
          and healthcare operations.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="research-area-img"><img src="images/proj-biomfg.jpg" alt="Digital twin and simulation model"></div>

        <h2>Simulation Optimization &amp; Explainable AI</h2>

          decision-making under uncertainty.</p>
